About me

Mr.

Boban Naumoski

Boban Naumoski is born 16 July 1990 in Ohrid, Macedonia. His first training was back in December 2004 in a local gym called Power House.

After 3 years of training he gained 25kg muscle mass and decided to try competing on a national level.

His first competition was "Athlete of Macedonia 2007" that was held in Kavadarci back in October 2007. He was only 17 years old and he won the show in a bodybuilding juniors category.

One week later he was competing at the "Figure of Macedonia 2007" that was held in his hometown Ohrid. He was the only junior on the stage and got the 1st place.

The next 3 years(2008,2009,2010) he continued competing at the national championship "Athlete of Macedonia" and he won all the shows, becoming a record holder by the number of titles(4) in bodybuilding juniors category in Macedonia, a record that it is still his.

In 2009 the bodybuilding and fitness federation of Macedonia(BBFFM) decided that he is ready to represent Macedonia as the first Macedonian junior on the IFBB World Juniors & Masters Championship that was held in Poland. He was the youngest competitor at the show(19 years old) and unluckily he didn't manage to enter in the final six.

In 2011 he was crowned as the "Most popular Macedonian bodybuilder for 2010" by the votes from the fans at the bodybuilding and fitness forum www.herkules.com.mk.

He started working as a professional fitness and bodybuilding trainer and nutritionist back in 2017 at the biggest fitness center in Ohrid - IB Fitness Center.

In May 2021, after he got the BBFFM Certificate for licensed personal trainer, he decided to create the most advanced web application by date for personalized client management - www.naumoski.fit
